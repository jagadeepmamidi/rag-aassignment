JOB TITLE: Senior Full Stack Developer

COMPANY: Razorpay
LOCATION: Bengaluru, Karnataka (Hybrid — 3 days WFO)
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-Time, Permanent
CTC RANGE: ₹25 LPA – ₹40 LPA (based on experience)

ABOUT RAZORPAY
Razorpay is India's leading full-stack financial solutions company. We are building the next generation of payment infrastructure for India and beyond. Our engineering team works on high-scale distributed systems processing millions of transactions daily.

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a Senior Full Stack Developer to join our Platform Engineering team. You will build and scale internal tools, merchant dashboards, and payment orchestration layers used by 10M+ businesses across India.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, develop, and maintain full-stack web applications using React and Node.js
- Build and optimize RESTful APIs and microservices architecture
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code with comprehensive unit and integration tests
- Collaborate with the product team to translate PRDs into technical solutions
- Mentor junior developers and conduct code reviews
- Participate in HLD/LLD discussions and contribute to architecture decisions
- Monitor application performance and implement optimizations
- Work with CI/CD pipelines and cloud infrastructure (AWS preferred)
- Participate in on-call rotation for production systems

MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
- B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science or equivalent from a recognized university
- 4+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in React.js (2+ years) and Node.js (3+ years)
- Experience with TypeScript in production applications
- Solid understanding of PostgreSQL or similar RDBMS
- Experience with RESTful API design and implementation
- Hands-on with Docker and container-based deployments
- Strong DSA fundamentals (will be tested in interviews)
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

GOOD TO HAVE
- Experience with AWS services (EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS)
- Knowledge of microservices and event-driven architecture
- Experience with GraphQL or gRPC
- Familiarity with Kubernetes and container orchestration
- Experience with Kafka or RabbitMQ
- Open-source contributions

INTERVIEW PROCESS
1. Online Assessment (DSA + MCQs) — 90 mins
2. Technical Round 1 — DSA + Problem Solving (60 mins)
3. Technical Round 2 — LLD + System Design (60 mins)
4. Technical Round 3 — HLD + Past Experience Deep Dive (60 mins)
5. Hiring Manager Round — Culture fit + Team alignment (45 mins)
