COMPANY: Swiggy
LOCATION: Bengaluru, Karnataka (Hybrid — 3 days WFO)
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-Time, Permanent

ABOUT SWIGGY
Swiggy is India's leading on-demand delivery platform, serving 500+ cities with food delivery, quick commerce (Instamart), and Dineout. Our engineering team builds systems that handle 2 Cr+ orders per month with 99.99% uptime.

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are hiring a Backend Engineer (SDE-2) for the Order Platform team. You will design and build APIs, data pipelines, and distributed systems that power Swiggy's core order lifecycle — from order placement to delivery completion — at massive scale.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and implement high-throughput RESTful and gRPC APIs
- Build and maintain data processing pipelines using Java/Python and streaming frameworks
- Optimize database performance across MySQL and NoSQL systems
- Implement and manage Kubernetes-orchestrated microservices
- Ensure system reliability through monitoring, alerting, and incident response
- Write technical design documents and participate in architecture reviews
- Collaborate with data science, ML, and frontend teams
- Contribute to infrastructure-as-code using Terraform

MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
- B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science or related field from a Tier-1/Tier-2 college
- 3+ years of backend development experience at a product company
- Strong proficiency in Java or Python
- Experience with MySQL/PostgreSQL and at least one NoSQL database (Cassandra, MongoDB, or Redis)
- Hands-on experience with Kubernetes and Docker in production
- Familiarity with message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ, or SQS)
- Experience with AWS or GCP cloud platform
- Understanding of microservices and distributed systems design patterns
- Strong DSA skills (will be evaluated in interview rounds)

GOOD TO HAVE
- Experience with Apache Kafka and real-time stream processing
- Knowledge of Apache Spark or Apache Flink
- Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Familiarity with Terraform or Pulumi
- Published research or open-source contributions
- Experience with observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog)
- Prior experience in food-tech, e-commerce, or logistics domain
